Our Services

WHAT WE OFFER

Diverse Range of Services

Solar On Grid Power System

We provide on-grid solar power systems that are directly connected to the utility grid, enabling users to generate clean energy and reduce electricity costs. These systems allow excess power to be exported to the grid through net metering

Solar Off Grid Power

Off-grid solar power systems operate independently without any connection to the utility grid. These systems use battery storage to supply power in remote and rural areas.

Hybrid Solar Power System

Hybrid solar power systems combine solar panels, battery storage, and grid supply to ensure uninterrupted power. These systems automatically switch between solar, battery, and grid based on availability.

Solar Power Plant Services

We provide complete design and engineering solutions for solar power plants, including site analysis and system planning.

Drone Development

We specialize in the design and development of advanced drone systems for applications such as surveillance, inspection, mapping and research.

Robotics Devices

We develop intelligent robotic solutions to automate logistics and material handling processes across industries.

RC Aircraft Design

We specialize in the design and development of radio-controlled (RC) aircraft for educational, research and recreational applications. Our work includes airframe design, aerodynamic analysis, propulsion system selection and flight control integration.

IoT Solutions

We develop smart IoT solutions that connect devices, sensors, and systems for real-time data monitoring and control. Our IoT applications include smart energy systems, industrial automation, home automation, and environmental monitoring.

Web Development

We provide professional web development services to create responsive, user-friendly, and high-performance websites. Our solutions include custom website design, web applications, and business portals tailored to client needs.
