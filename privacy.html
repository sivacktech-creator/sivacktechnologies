Privacy Policy

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register for training, sign up for membership, or contact us. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and educational or professional background.

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your data is stored securely, and we take reasonable industry-standard precautions to protect it from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
